The short version

I'm a Computer Science student at Ain Shams University and a game developer based in Cairo, Egypt. I like the parts of game dev that sit right between a programming puzzle and a craft: getting a multiplayer tank battle to feel instant online, teaching an enemy to actually aim at you, or making a VR scene comfortable enough that people forget they're wearing a headset.

Most of what's in the store came out of internships, solo weekend projects, or a game jam with a two-day deadline and not nearly enough sleep. I've shipped a multiplayer shooter, a couple of 2D games, a level generator, and worked on VR comfort systems for a studio — and picked up a first-place win along the way.

When I'm not writing gameplay code, I'm usually somewhere adjacent to it — editing trailers, running workshops, or helping other students make their first game.
